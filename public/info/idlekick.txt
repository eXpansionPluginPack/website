[h1]IdleKick[/h1]
Kicks non-playing players from the server automagically.

[h1]how to enable[/h1]
add following line to [code]<ManiaLive>\config\config.ini[/code]
[source]
manialive.plugins[] = 'eXpansion\IdleKick'
[/source]

[h1]Configuration options[/h1]
[h2]Maximum idling time[/h2]
Number of minutes after which the player will be kicked

[b]Var Name : [/b]idleMinutes (int)
[h3]Default Configuration[/h3]
[code]ManiaLivePlugins\eXpansion\IdleKick\idleMinutes[] = 10
[/code]
