[h1]IdleKick[/h1]
Kicks non-playing players from the server automagically.

[h1]how to enable[/h1]
add following line to [code]<ManiaLive>\config\config.ini[/code]
[source]
manialive.plugins[] = 'eXpansion\IdleKick'
[/source]

[h1]Configuration options[/h1]
you can alter the plugin options by adding these lines to [code]ManiaLive\config\config.ini[/code]

[source]
ManiaLivePlugins\eXpansion\IdleKick\Config.idleMinutes = 10

[/source]
