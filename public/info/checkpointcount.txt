[h1]CheckpointCounter[/h1]
s a widget familiar from XAseco, simply displays current checkpoint at bottom of the screen

[h1]how to enable[/h1]
add following line to [code]ManiaLive\config\config.ini[/code]

[source]manialive.plugins[] = 'eXpansion\CheckpointCounter[/source]

[h1]Configuration options[/h1]
none.
