[h1]Widgets_Record[/h1]
shows dedimania and localrecords on TAB-layer (by default) or as widget on normal layer at left edge of screen

[h1]how to enable[/h1]
autoloaded.

[h1]Configuration options[/h1]
[source]
ManiaLivePlugins\eXpansion\Widgets_Record\Config.layer = 'score'   ;;normal or score
[/source]
