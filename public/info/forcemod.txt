[h1]ForceMod[/h1]
enables a custom mod-file to be forced at server.

[h1]how to enable[/h1]
add following line to [code]<ManiaLive>\config\config.ini[/code]
[source]
manialive.plugins[] = 'eXpansion\ForceMod'
[/source]
[h1]Configuration options[/h1]
[h2]mods[/h2]
url address of the mod .zip file. example: http://reaby.kapsi.fi/ml/mods/xmas2.zip

[b]Var Name : [/b]mods (array( string))
[h3]Default Configuration[/h3]
[code]ManiaLivePlugins\eXpansion\ForceMod\mods[] = 
ManiaLivePlugins\eXpansion\ForceMod\mods[] = 
ManiaLivePlugins\eXpansion\ForceMod\mods[] = 
[/code]
