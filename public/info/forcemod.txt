[h1]ForceMod[/h1]
enables a custom mod-file to be forced at server.

[h1]how to enable[/h1]
add following line to [code]<ManiaLive>\config\config.ini[/code]
[source]
manialive.plugins[] = 'eXpansion\ForceMod'
[/source]
[h1]Configuration options[/h1]
you can alter the plugin options by adding these lines to [code]ManiaLive\config\config.ini[/code]

[source]
;;mandatory
ManiaLivePlugins\eXpansion\ForceMod\Config.mods[] = "http://your_server.com/mod1.zip"
ManiaLivePlugins\eXpansion\ForceMod\Config.mods[] = "http://your_server.com/mod2.zip"

;; optional
ManiaLivePlugins\eXpansion\ForceMod\Config.loadscreen = "http://your_server.com/loadscreen.png"
[/source]
