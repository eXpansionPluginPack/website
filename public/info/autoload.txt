[h1]AutoLoad[/h1]

AutoLoad is the easiest way to get full pluginpack up and running without any hassle, just add one line and you are good to go.

[h1]how to enable[/h1]

add following line to [code]ManiaLive\config\config.ini[/code]
[source]manialive.plugins[] = 'eXpansion\Autoload'
[/source]

[h1]Configuration options[/h1]
[h2]Disabling a Plugin[/h2]
Set plugins to disable

[b]Var Name : [/b]disable (array( string))
[h3]Default Configuration[/h3]
[code]ManiaLivePlugins\eXpansion\AutoLoad\disable[] = 
[/code]

Allows you to disable plugins that are automatically loaded by the Autoloader
