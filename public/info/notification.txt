[h1]Notifications[/h1]
a 2nd chat-like window with 6 scrolling lines to redirect different plugins server messages

[h1]how to enable[/h1]
add following line to [code]<ManiaLive>\config\config.ini[/code]
[source]
manialive.plugins[] = 'eXpansion\Notifications'
[/source]

[h1]Configuration options[/h1]
none.
