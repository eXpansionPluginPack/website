[h1]Quiz[/h1]
run a quiz with points counting, powered by /ask command, no predefined questions data, players ask and answer theirself

chatcommands:
/ask
/answer
/point
/points
/question
/cancel

[h1]how to enable[/h1]
add following line to [code]<ManiaLive>\config\config.ini[/code]
[source]
manialive.plugins[] = 'eXpansion\Quiz'
[/source]

[h1]Configuration options[/h1]
none.
